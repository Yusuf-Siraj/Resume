Exploring AI in Health & Wellness

August 2025

AI is transforming how we approach health and wellness, from personalized nutrition plans to tailored fitness recommendations. Building my Health & Wellness AI app gave me a firsthand look at the challenges and rewards of combining technical functionality with user-centric design.

Project Overview

The app leverages AI to provide personalized health recommendations. Users input preferences and lifestyle data, and the system generates guidance on nutrition, exercise, and wellness habits. This project strengthened my skills in Java, Python, Spring Boot, and Vue.js.

Challenges Faced

  • Integrating AI models while ensuring fast response times for users.
  • Designing an intuitive user interface that simplifies complex health data.
  • Handling and storing sensitive user information securely.
  • Managing the project solo while learning new frameworks and libraries on the fly.

Key Learnings

  • Rapidly navigating unfamiliar tech documentation to implement AI features efficiently.
  • Balancing technical depth with usability to create meaningful user experiences.
  • Project management skills like scoping, backlog prioritization, and rapid prototyping directly impact development speed and quality.

Next Steps

I plan to enhance the app by integrating more advanced AI models and expanding functionality to support holistic wellness tracking. The experience reinforced my passion for building impactful tech and solidified my interest in software engineering, product management, and innovative digital solutions. COMING SOON!
